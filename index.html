What the app does

The app prepares media packages, drafts captions, organizes posting queues, and supports publishing flows for connected platforms where the user has provided authorization and required credentials.

Who can use it

The service is intended for adults managing their own brand assets or creator operations, or for teams acting with clear permission from the account owner.

Compliance first

Users must comply with applicable law, intellectual property rules, privacy rights, and each platform's policies. The service must not be used to impersonate real people, deceive users, or target minors with age-restricted material.

Service overview

Clara Rubi Automation is an operational toolset for creator media management. It can help prepare post-ready assets, maintain a content schedule, and coordinate review steps before supported uploads. Some features may rely on third-party APIs and require the user's separate approval inside those platforms.
